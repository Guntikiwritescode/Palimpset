# Owner outreach — ready-to-send drafts

These are the lead-time actions from HANDOFF §2.4 that **only the owner can send**
(they are outreach to third parties). They are drafted here so sending is a
copy-paste; fill in the bracketed fields. The implementer does **not** send them
and does **not** fabricate any confirmation — the license gate stays closed until
a real written answer arrives (`source.license_confirmed=false`).

| Draft | Unblocks | Lead time |
|---|---|---|
| `sdfb-license-confirmation.md` | Ingress / public deployment / published datasets / the "real data" demo claim | weeks; may never arrive |
| `folger-data-request.md` | typed predicates, sub-year date precision, **source-native competing life dates** | weeks |
| `emlo-permission-request.md` | nothing in Part 1 (correspSearch is the P1 layer); an EMLO adapter only if granted | weeks |

The Folger request is the highest-value: an *alternate birth/death year per person*
is the source-native competing claim §20 A1 found the Part 1 corpus to lack.
correspSearch (WP2b, now landed) is the interim second source; the Folger export
and a licence-confirmed SDFB are what turn the demo's "real data" claim on.
